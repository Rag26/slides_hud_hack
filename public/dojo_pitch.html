01Why Now

For AI to do its own research, it must build its own environments.

AI can only self-improve if it can author its own experiments — and in RL, an experiment is an environment.

self-improvement→ author experiments→ in RL, that’s an environment
02Introducing Dojo

An environment that trains a model to build environments.

Meet Sensei: an LLM that learns to generate RL environments by training inside one. Its students are small Players.

Sensei (LLM) designs an RL environment
↓
Players (RL agents) train in it
↓
players beat baselines → win-ratio= Sensei’s reward
↺
update Sensei → repeat
03How Sensei Learns

Players train in Sensei’s environment, then fight baseline players.

01Ring-Out DueltrainKnock the opponent off the platform.
02Target KnockbacktrainPunch the opponent into a target zone.
03King of the Hillfinal examHold the zone longest. Never trained on.

Players’ win-ratio vs. baselines = Sensei’s reward.

04How It Works

Two nested loops, then a fair exam.

05See It Run

Watch Sensei train a player, live.

06Reward Hacking Mitigation

You can’t fake a player getting better.

  • Tested on arenas Sensei didn’t pick.
  • Must win a game it never trained on.
07The Result

Sensei’s player won.
